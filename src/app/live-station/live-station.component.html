Live Station:

As a passenger, you might be interesting about to get to know details about all the trains that arrives and departs from particular station. Suppose, if you are in secunderabad (SC) station and you want to know all the trains that pass from that station, Railway Enquiry is here for you. Bu just entering station code and hours in which you would like to see the trains, railway enquiry will display all the trains that arrive and depart from one particular station.

Just follow the following simple steps:

  1. Go to railwayenquiry.net
  2. Click on “RAIN DETAILS”
  3. lick on “LIVE STATION”
  4. Enter “Station Name”
  5. Select Time in 2 hours or 4 hours
  6. Click on Search

That’s it, all the trains that arrives and departs from particular station is in front of you.
