How to check rescheduled trains:

  1. Go to railwayenquiry.net
  2. Click on TRAIN DETAILS drop down
  3. Click on RESCHEDULED Trains
  4. Select the DATE when you would like to check the rescheduled trains list.
  5. Click on SEARCH button

That is it the rescheduled trains list for the particular date is in front of you.

Frequently Asked Questions:

  1. Can I cancel the ticket for rescheduled train?

    Yes. If your train is delayed more than 3 hours of actual departure time you can cancel your reserved ticket.

  2. Can I get refund of I cancel the ticket for rescheduled train?

    Yes. If your train is delayed more than 3 hours of actual departure time then you cancel the ticket. You will get refund.

  3. How do I get refund I cancel ticket for rescheduled train?

    You need to file a TDR (Ticket Deposit Receipt) specifying that the train is rescheduled, then you will be refunded in 15 days.
